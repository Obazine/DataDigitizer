Data Digitizer Quickstart Guide

  • 1. On the side bar, hover over to the Images tab and upload an image of the graph you want to work with.
  • 2. Just below the images tab, click on "Axes Calibration", and click on the minimum x value, maximum x value, minimum y value and maximum x value respectively.
  • 3. Once the axes calibration is complete, a pop-up form will appear, prompting you to enter the values for the respective points.
  • 4. Hover over the Measurements tab and click on "Get Points Values" to mark out the points where you want to get your values from.
  • 5. Finally, hover over to the Datasets tab and click "Export Data", this will download a csv file containing all the points you have marked.
